• Pigs in a Blanket

    Best of all, they only require three ingredients, hot dogs, crescent rolls, and American cheese, and take less than 30 minutes to make.

  • Chicken Wings

    These chicken wings may be messy, but they taste so good, no one will mind. The chicken is juicy, tender, and fried to perfection.

  • Mac and Cheese Bites

    These bite-sized balls of yum have crunchy, garlicky outsides and gooey, cheesy insides. They take over an hour to make, but they are always the biggest hit at the party.

  • Crispy Onion Rings

    They have extraordinarily crispy, crunchy outsides, and the onions within are soft, tender, and tasty. The smoked paprika in the recipe also gives them a kick, which makes them even better.
